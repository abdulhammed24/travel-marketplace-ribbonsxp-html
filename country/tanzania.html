Stunning Beaches: Zanzibar is famous for its white sandy beaches and crystal clear waters. Perfect for relaxing or water sports.


Rich History: Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is rich in history and culture. You’ll find a mix of Arab, Persian, Indian and European influences.


Delicious Food: Known as the Spice Island, Zanzibar offers a variety of dishes full of flavor. Don’t miss out on the seafood!


Wildlife: You can spot dolphins, turtles, and a variety of tropical fish. There’s also Jozani Forest, home to the Red Colobus Monkey.
